# Community Projects

The Community Development Initiative Proposal (CDIP) is *_the_* scoping and framing tool for anyone interested in receiving funding for proposing new MakerDAO community initiatives or resources. 


If you are looking to contribute to an existing project, head over to our <Link to="{../../work-with-us/bounties/index.mdx}">bounties page</Link>

## Propose Your Idea

A proposal should combine your ideas and skills with our shared goal of improving the resources for the MakerDAO community. Follow these steps to start your proposal.

### 1. Write your proposal using our template.

Use our Google Doc template to help you structure your proposal so that we get the information we need.

<Link to="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1lF8mNuomrguIS3lnvqTomS4NdON40nuyKRbLS6ZkMoA/edit">Open proposal template</Link>


### 2. Post your proposal in our chat.

Post your proposal in our Community Development chat. An Advisor will reach out to help you through next steps.

<Link to="https://chat.makerdao.com/channel/community-development" icon="rocketchat">Community chat</Link>

An Approving Group meets weekly to review proposals and will be in touch for regular updates. For more detail, read about the [community project process](./community-projects-process.md).

<CTA>

If you are unsure about your proposal, post your idea in the chat for feedback. An Advisor will help you iterate on your proposal.

</CTA>

## Dos and Don'ts

<CTA>

<Box>
**DO**

- Check existing or past [proposals](https://github.com/makerdao/community/issues?q=label%3ACDIP+)
- Spend 1-3 hours on properly fleshing out your proposal
- Bring friends and colleagues in to help if necessary

</Box>

</CTA>

<CTA>

<Box>
**DON'T**

- Invoice for work before approval, you may not be paid for it
- Submit more than one proposal at a time
- Duplicate an existing proposal
- Submit a proposal for small pieces of work

</Box>

</CTA>
